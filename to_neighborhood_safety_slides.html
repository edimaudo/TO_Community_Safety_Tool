TO Neighborhood Safety Tool

Driving Community Safety in Toronto

Overview

Why

The TO Neighborhood Safety Tool was created to help residents understand socio-economic information and crime incidents across Toronto neighborhoods. It provides insights into trends and provides the ability to predict the type of incident, forecast future levels of incidents as well as provide safety tieps to support safer communities.
